Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new job?

Tap the + button in the top right corner of any screen. Fill in the client name, address, job description, and time. The app will automatically save and show it on your map and schedule.

How do I navigate to a job?

On the Map tab, tap on a job marker to see the job details, then tap the "Navigate" button. This will open Apple Maps with driving directions to the client's address.

How do I track my income?

Go to the Income tab to see your total earnings. You can switch between weekly and monthly views. Only completed jobs are counted in your income total.

How do I sync my data across devices?

Go to Settings and enable iCloud Sync. Your data will automatically sync across all devices signed into the same iCloud account.

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription through your iPhone Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions. You can also restore purchases in the RouteSnap Settings.

Is my data backed up?

If you enable iCloud Sync, your data is automatically backed up to your iCloud account. Without iCloud Sync, your data is only stored locally on your device.
